Jieru Mei

I am a fourth-year PhD student in Computer Science at Johns Hopkins University, advised by Bloomberg Distinguished Professor Dr. Alan Yuille. I am a member of Computational Cognition, Vision, and Learning.

Before that, I obtained B.S. in Automation at Huazhong University of Science and Technology in 2018. I also spent time at Waymo, Google Brain, Tencent, ByteDance, and MSRA.
